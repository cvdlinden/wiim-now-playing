# What is WiiM Now Playing?

WiiM Now Playing transforms any secondary screen into a dedicated music dashboard.

It extracts live metadata and album art directly from your WiiM device to provide an elegant, glanceable display for your living room, desk, or audio rack.

![All screens](../assets/all-screens.jpg)

## Features

* **Live Metadata Sync**  
  *Shows the current track, artist and album from your WiiM streamer.*
* **Album Art**  
  *Automatically fetches the album art for an integrated display.*
* **Source and Audio Quality Identification**  
  *Immediately see your streaming service and exact audio quality.*
* **Responsive Layout**  
  *Shines on every screen, from 7 inches (or smaller) up until 70 inches (or larger).*
* **Multi-WiiM Device Support**  
  *Quickly switch between multiple WiiM devices on your network.*
* **Multi-Client Sync**  
  *A single server can sync the now-playing status to multiple screens at once.*
* **Quick Controls**  
  *Easily pause, play, skip or rewind tracks.*
* **Volume Control**  
  *Change the device volume on the fly directly from the interface.*
* **Playlist Shortcuts**  
  *Quickly launch your favorite predefined playlists.*
* **TV Mode**  
  *A minimalistic interface designed to keep the focus entirely on the track and artwork.*
* **Synced Lyrics**  
  *Automatically fetches and displays real-time lyrics for the current track.*
